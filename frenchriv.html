Trip to the French Riviera

KWEST trip to Nice, Cannes, and Monaco!

Nice
Nice receives over 4 million visitors a year who come to relax and soak up the sun and natural beauty. It does not disappoint, with beautiful beaches that stretch on forever and perfectly blue water.

Cannes
Cannes is best known for its film festival, but it has it fair share of beaches as well. It is also the jumping off point for Ile Saint-Honorat, a uniquely beautiful island which has been home to a community of monks since the 5th century (monastery pictured).

Boat day
We got to spend a great day on a catamaran cruising off the coast of Cannes with a lunchtime break for some swimming, kayaking, and paddle boarding.

Monaco
Monaco is just a short train ride from Nice and its population is home to a larger percent of millionaires than Zurich or Geneva (see the yachts in the harbor). Because of the high cliffs behind it, the city was highly valued as a strategic asset.
