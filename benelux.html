Trip to Benelux

Christmas break trip to Belegium, Luxembourg, and the Netherlands

Bruges and Ghent
We spent the day driving to Bruges (pictured) along tree-lined canals. After exploring Bruges and the Christmas market, we drove to Ghent for the night.

Brussels
After driving from Ghent to Brussels in the early morning, we spent the day exploring Belgium's capital. We saw the palace (pictured), Christmas fair, main town square and finished off the night with some Belgian beers.

Luxembourg
In Luxembourg, we explored the Grund quarter, the lower level of the city below the fancy shops and castle. After climbing up the hill to the upper levels, we stopped in for another Christmas fair, completed with mulled wine.

Amsterdam
After making the long drive from Luxembourg to Amsterdam and stopping in Cologne, Germany along the way, we settled in to our Airbnb in the Centrum. We spent the next few days exploring Amsterdam's canals (pictured), museums, and restaurants.
